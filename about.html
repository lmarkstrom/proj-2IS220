About Recipe Reviewer

Recipe Reviewer is a website where you can find and share recipes with near and dears. You can also review recipes and share your experiences with others so that others can find the new fantastic recipes. Find new tastes and explore new dishes.

Find exciting and most reviewed recipes on the internet. Se what other think and see the average rating of the recipe.

How to use

To use Recipe Reviewer you can search for recipes in the search bar. You can also click on the reviews tab to see the most reviewed recipes. You can also click on the about tab to read more about Recipe Reviewer.

When reciving the search result you can click on the recipe to see more information about the recipe. You can can see the ingredients, the instructions and the average rating of the recipe. You can also choose to review the recipe and share your experience with others.

In the reviews tab you can see the most reviewed recipes on the internet. You can see the average rating of the different recipes and you can click on the recipe to see more information about the recipe.

About Edamam

Edamam is a website that provides recipes and nutrition information. The website has a large database of recipes and nutrition information that can be used by other websites. The website also provides an API that can be used to retrieve recipes and nutrition information from the website.

Edamam is free for development purposes and can be used by anyone who wants to retrieve recipes and nutrition information from the website. The website also provides a paid version that can be used for commercial purposes.

As this website is created as part of a course and there is no commercial purpose with the website the free version of Edamam is used.
